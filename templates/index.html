MCDA Decision Support System

A web-based tool for evaluating and comparing companies using multiple decision-making methods.

1️⃣ Select Companies

Choose companies from the database and assign weights to evaluation criteria such as revenue, profit, assets, growth of those, employees and ROA .

Go to Selection

2️⃣ Apply Methods

Analyze companies with different MCDA methods: WSM, TOPSIS, PROMETHEE I, and VIKOR. Each method provides a ranking and interpretation.

Try Methods

3️⃣ Compare Results

Compare rankings from all methods on a single page and identify the most stable and reliable decision where to invest your money.


View Results
This system supports multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA). It helps managers and researchers evaluate alternatives from different perspectives and make better, data-driven choices.
